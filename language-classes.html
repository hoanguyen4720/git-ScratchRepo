Our Vietnamese language programs

We offer specialized Vietnamese language classes designed to meet the needs of diverse learners, fostering confidence and a deeper connection to Vietnamese culture. Our approach focuses on practical communication and cultural immersion.

Youth (6–12)

Fun, interactive classes introducing basic Vietnamese vocabulary and phrases.

Teens (13–17)

Conversational skills and cultural understanding for teenage learners.

Adults (18+)

Beginner to advanced courses emphasizing practical communication.

Connect with Vietnamese culture and identity

Language is more than words — it’s identity, history, and belonging.

Class levels and structure

Beginner: Basic vocabulary, tones, simple conversations, reading & writing fundamentals.

Intermediate: Grammar, sentence structure, reading passages, cultural expressions.

Advanced: Complex conversations, storytelling, literature, writing reflections.

What to expect

  • Stronger speaking and listening skills
  • Confidence using Vietnamese in daily life
  • Connection to Vietnamese culture
  • Community friendships

Classes are held in-person, with 2 sessions of 10–12 week courses. Each class lasts 150 minutes.
