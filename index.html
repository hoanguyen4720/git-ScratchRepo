Our Mission & Values

We aim to nurture a strong sense of cultural identity among Vietnamese youth growing up abroad by helping them stay connected to their roots. Through a thoughtful and engaging curriculum, we give students meaningful opportunities to learn about the Vietnamese language, history, and cultural traditions in ways that feel relevant, welcoming, and empowering.

Our Core Offerings

We provide a range of specialized services and programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our community, focusing on education, cultural enrichment, and engagement opportunities.

Vietnamese language classes

Engaging classes for all ages and proficiency levels, designed to teach Vietnamese language skills effectively.

Cultural programs & workshops

Immersive experiences that explore Vietnamese traditions, history, art, and culinary heritage.

Volunteer & teaching opportunities

Opportunities for individuals, including older adults, to contribute their skills and time to our community.

Upcoming events & news

Stay updated on our latest news, cultural celebrations, and community gatherings.

About the Vietnamese American Community Center

Established with a passion for preserving and promoting Vietnamese culture, our center serves as a unique bridge between generations and cultures in the DMV area. We are a trusted resource for Vietnamese language education, cultural exchange, and community building, welcoming everyone to experience our rich heritage.
